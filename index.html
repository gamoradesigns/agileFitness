OUR PROMISE

Fitness just for you

Agile Fitness promises to tailor your fitness journey. Agile by definition means able to move quickly and easily. That is the promise. At every twist and turn agile’s educated passionate fitness consultants will be there to tailor your fitness journey, goal, and path.

Our mission

Our mission is to tailor your fitness journey that complements your lifestyle’s needs, desire, and demands. Fitness is an enjoyable experience for everyone. Our dedicated team of consultants and personal trainers are here with you on the journey every step of the way!

Powerlifting

Build strength with our powerlifting circuit. Powerlifting is a strength sport that consists of three attempts at maximal weight on: squat, bench press, and deadlift. Our trainers are skilled in form and safety.

Zumba

We offer a fun 1 hour Zumba class. The more you enjoy your exercise routine, the more likely you are to stick with it. Many people say they have so much fun dancing that they forget they are actually exercising.

Yoga & Meditation

From yoga to guided meditation, we offer a practice where an individual uses a technique to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.

Swimming

Swimming is an excellent way to work your entire body and cardiovascular system. An hour of swimming burns almost as many calories as running, without all the impact on your bones and joints.
